The Haukai
Welcome to The Haukai, where we offer a taste of exotic local cuisine that will transport you to the lush landscapes of the Pacific Islands. Our menu is bursting with fresh ingredients and bold flavors that are sure to tantalize your taste buds. From tender seafood dishes to hearty meat options and vegetarian delights, we have something for everyone. Indulge in our succulent octopus ceviche or savor our juicy lamb shank in rich coconut cream. Our dishes are prepared with passion and served with warmth, creating an atmosphere that is truly unforgettable. And to accompany your meal, we offer an extensive wine and cocktail list, featuring both local and international favorites. So come and experience the magic of Haukai, where the food, the people, and the ambiance will leave you feeling invigorated and inspired. We can't wait to welcome you!
